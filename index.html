How to use hemp

    Decarboxylate it to make the cannabinoids biologically active. Easy to do! Just heat the hemp for 35 min in an oven set at 245 degrees F.
    Incorporate the decarboxylated into edibles like brownies, cookies, or use it like a spice where an earthy musk would complement the dish. Blends into curry well. Make sure to weigh out an approprate dose using a sensitive scale, thinking about the potency of what you're using.
    Make your own extract at home to stimulate your economy! Take your decarboxylated hemp, mix with 100g coconut oil per 10g biomass (feel free to adjust based on potency of hemp), add to a slow cooker, and heat on the lowest setting for 3 hours. I've seen some extracts add water to make sure the temperature never goes over the boiling point of water. After 3 hours strain the plant material out, and let the oil cool. If you added water, you can cool in the fridge, and when the oil is solid you can remove it and pat dry. Great for adding to food, or topical applications. Nice.
